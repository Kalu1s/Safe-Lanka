11 Powerful Ways We Protect You

Swipe to explore our comprehensive suite of safety tools.

1. Crime SOS

Instantly alert police. One tap sends your ID and geolocation to the nearest control room.

2. Traffic SOS

Report accidents or road hazards immediately. Helps police clear roads faster.

3. Live GPS Tracking

Share your real-time movement with police while an incident is active.

4. Nearby Police Station Locator

Tracks your live location and provide you with the nearest police stations.

5. Anonymous Reporting Portal

Allows the citizen to report crimes anonymously for safety.

6. Verified Identity

Integrates with NIC system to prevent fake reports and ensure accountability.

7. Traffic Fine Viewing & Payment

Check your traffic fines instantly and make secure online payments without visiting a police station.

8. Multi language Support

Full support for Sinhala, Tamil, and English languages.

9. Crime and Complaint Reporting

Report crimes quickly with photos, videos, and location details.

10. Emergency Contacts

Automatically notify your trusted family members when you use SOS.

11. Lost and Found Reporting Portal

Report lost or found items easily and help connect belongings with their rightful owners.

12. Case Tracking & Status Updates

Monitor the progress of your cases through a unique case ID and receive timely updates from the police.
